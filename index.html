About me

I'm a dynamic and versatile Technical Program Manager and Front-End Engineer with 3.5+ years of experience excelling in high-stakes environments. My passion lies in transforming chaos into structured efficient deliveries and bridging the gap between strategic vision and technical execution.

I thrive on aligning executive stakeholders' complex requirements and translating them into actionable plans to drive full lifecycle implementation of complex, scalable web applications and products. My expertise includes personally building core site functionalities and custom components on platforms like Oracle Content Management System (OCM), a CMS/ Digital Asset Manager (DAM) system I leveraged for client projects.

I was then chosen as a Subject Matter Expert (SME) for creating a new major Content Management System (CMS) / DAM, where I provided architectural and development guidance, owning defect logging and retesting, identifying product gaps, and managing sprint prioritization for continuous enhancement. I led new deployments and rollouts of campaigns for international clients, optimizing site performance and security with advanced infrastructure (CDNs, Cloudflare, Oracle Cloud Infrastructure), and achieving quantifiable marketing and operational improvements using platforms like Eloqua, and Oracle CX. I also am interested in Generative AI, holding relevant certifications.

Technically adept at debugging and highly skilled at problem-solving. I’m driven to lead and empower teams, ensuring everything we deliver achieves exceptional quality and impactful results. I’m truly passionate about making a significant impact by calming the chaos within technical programs, building stable and scalable technical architectures, and cultivating cross-functional teamwork and delivery.

Let’s connect if you’re looking for a Technical Project Manager who’s just as comfortable in the codebase as they are in the client meeting, and who consistently transforms ambitious visions into well-ordered, shippable realities.
